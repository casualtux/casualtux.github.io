Casual Tux Flowers and Leaves

Casual was created to be the logo for Yor Linux. He was originally designed to be recognizable across a crowded conference room. Thus his flowers had to be bold and bright. That also explains the names of the flowers.

Although I loved the original Casual Tux, his flowers were not really Hawaiian. I wanted to make a real Hawaiian shirt, and needed the right flower for it. After looking at many Hawaiian shirts I settled on the Hibiscus flower.
I have documented more of how I made the hibiscus flower in the making of an ultimate hawaiian shirt - flowers.
