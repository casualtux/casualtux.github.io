Casual Tux Hibiscus

I decided I needed the ultimate Hawaiian Linux shirt. The front will be a nice Hawaiian shirt, and the back will have a large Casual Tux sitting in the middle.
Although I love Casual Tux, his flowers are rather simplistic and not Hawaiian. After looking at many Hawaiian shirts I settled on the Hibiscus flower.
So I designed Casual Tux Hibiscus.

I am documenting the whole design process of my ultimate on my blog.
