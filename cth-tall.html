Pattern: CTH (Casual Tux Hibiscus) Tall

When I started trying to put the CTH Square pattern on fabric, I realized that my square pattern wasn't going to give me the affect that I really wanted. I would simply have stripes of flowers, or stripes of flowers and Casual Tuxes.
So I made the pattern taller. Two times taller. With the pattern filled with leaves and flowers.
